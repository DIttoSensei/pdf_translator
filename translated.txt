Brinkwood na a town for kingdom of Eldoria, na famous for lively festival wey dey hold there and welcoming atmosphere. But Brinkwood get one dark history of pikin wey dey disappear for annual harvest festival. Despite investigation by Eldorian knights, dem no get any lead and pikin disappearance continue. Even festival dey put on hold for five year, but problem persist. Now, one anonymous person ask guild of adventurers to investigate disappearances, but past attempt to do am result in disappearance of adventurers self, and raise more suspicion.
